About Alpha Academy

Pioneering Excellence in Education Since 1996

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become leaders in their chosen fields.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that shapes the future of education through innovation, excellence, and student success.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

From humble beginnings to becoming a center of educational excellence

Founded in 1996 by Prof. Imran Qamar Sandhu, Alpha Academy has grown from a small coaching center to a comprehensive educational institution. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has helped thousands of students achieve their academic and professional goals.

Today, we continue to expand our programs and facilities while maintaining the highest standards of education and student support.

Our Journey

Alpha Academy Timeline

Key milestones in our journey of educational excellence

1996

Foundation

Alpha Academy was established by Prof. Imran Qamar Sandhu as a small tutoring center in Gujranwala.

2000

Expansion

Expanded to a larger facility to accommodate growing student enrollment and introduced new academic programs.

2005

Technology Integration

Introduced computer labs and began offering technology courses, including basic computer skills and web development.

2010

Home Tuition Services

Launched personalized home tuition services to provide one-on-one instruction for students requiring individual attention.

2015

Digital Transformation

Implemented online learning platforms and digital resources to enhance the learning experience.

2020

Global Recognition

Received international recognition for excellence in education and innovative teaching methodologies.
